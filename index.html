ABOUT US

The Weekend Steam Boost is a technology driven training program for kids of the ages 7 to 17 which has been running for over 5 months now and has been moving on greatly as exuded by the enthusiasm and passion shown by the kids to explore, discover, learn, create, and innovate with the various technologies presented to them. This program utilizes a project-based and non-linear learning approach for practical transmission and understanding of the technologies.
